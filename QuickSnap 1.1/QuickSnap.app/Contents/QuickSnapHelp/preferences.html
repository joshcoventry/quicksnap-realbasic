QuickSnap Help Index:Preferences

Preferences

The Preferences window is where the bulk of QuickSnap's functionality is. The Preferences window lets you define how QuickSnap takes screenshots, where they are saved, in what format, what they are named, and so on.
Using the Preferences window

There are several sections in the Preferences window:

  • General, for options relating to QuickSnap itself and screenshot files.
  • Capture, for options relating to the way screenshots are taken.
  • Hot Keys, for turning Hot Keys on and off.
  • Software Update, for checking for new updates.
  • Register, to enter your license information after purchasing QuickSnap.
How do I delete my settings?
To delete the QuickSnap settings, you must delete the Preferences file located at <Your Home Folder>/Library/Preferences/QuickSnap Prefs. Deleting this file will remove all of the settings, including your license information if you have purchased QuickSnap.
