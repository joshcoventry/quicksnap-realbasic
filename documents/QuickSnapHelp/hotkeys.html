QuickSnap Help Index:Hot Keys

Hot Keys

Hot Keys are keyboard shortcuts which can be used in any application, not just QuickSnap. They allow you to use and access QuickSnap's features system-wide for convenience.
Turning Hot Keys on
  1. Open the Preferences window by clicking on the Options button in the QuickSnap window.
  2. Click on the Hot Keys icon.
  3. Check the Hot Keys checkbox to turn them on.
  4. Open the System Preferences by choosing System Preferences from the Apple menu.
  5. Navigate to the Keyboard preferences.
  6. Click on the Keyboard Shortcuts tab.
  7. Click on the checkbox for 'Screen Shots'. This turns off Apple's default screenshot keyboard shortcuts, allowing QuickSnap's hot keys to replace them.
Having trouble activating the Hot Keys?
If you are having any difficulty turning off Apple's default hot keys to enable QuickSnap's, e-mail us and we will assist you the best we can.
Can I define my own custom Hot Keys?
Currently, QuickSnap does not have customizable Hot Keys.
