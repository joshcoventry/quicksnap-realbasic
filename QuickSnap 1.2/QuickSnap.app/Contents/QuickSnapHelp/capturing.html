Capturing

Capturing the screen, a selection or window is very easy.
Taking a screenshot
In the main QuickSnap window, single-click on a screenshot mode (Screen, Selection or Window). To take the screenshot, click the Capture button. The screenshot will then be taken in the particular way defined in the Preferences window, which can be accessed by clicking the Options button.
Can I take a screenshot using a keyboard shortcut?
Yes. You can use the keyboard shortcuts under the Capture menu to take screenshots in QuickSnap.
How do I change the way QuickSnap takes my screenshots?
The capture options are in the Preferences window which can be accessed by choosing Preferences from the QuickSnap menu, or by clicking the Options button on the QuickSnap window. Alternatively, the keyboard shortcut Cmd+, can be used to open the Preferences window.
