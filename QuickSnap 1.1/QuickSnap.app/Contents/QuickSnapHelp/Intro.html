QuickSnap Help Index:Introduction

Introduction

QuickSnap is an easy and fast utility for taking screenshots of your screen, a selection or a window.

Main features:

  • Take a screenshot of the screen, a selection, window, menu or icon.
  • Access QuickSnap from anywhere with hot keys and a status menu.
  • Save screenshots in multiple image formats.
  • Turn capturing sounds on and off.
  • Take timed screenshots.
Can QuickSnap capture what's on my screen as a movie?
No, QuickSnap does not currently have this feature and only allows you to take screenshots in a still image format.
Can QuickSnap completely replace the standard way of taking screenshots?
Yes. Using the hot keys, QuickSnap can replace Apple's standard way of taking screenshots using Cmd+Shift+3. See the Hot Keys page for more information.
